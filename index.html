Welcome to the Rural Employment Insights

The MGNREGA District Dashboard helps citizens easily understand how their district is performing under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) program. It visualizes official government data through interactive charts, making it simple to track employment, expenditure, and progress across rural India.

This initiative aims to bridge the data gap and empower rural citizens by converting complex government statistics into easy, meaningful insights.
